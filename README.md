# Khunegos

Khunegos, a new Minecraft SMP oriented PvP, inspired by LifeSteal SMP.

You have one hour to prepare to the Khunegos, an event that will divide you into two groups: the hunters and the preys.
Each hunter has one prey to kill.
If they succeed, they will gain one heart, and the prey will lose five hearts.
If they failed, they will lose two hearts, and they will have to retry during the next Khunegos, but, this time, the 
hunter will have another to task to accomplish: survive like a prey or kill someone.

A new Khunegos adds a new task to the previous one⋅s every 20 minutes.

But this task will be too easy for the hunter.
They do not have access to the name of their prey.
They only have access to their coordinates.
If it's too hard, they can reveal themselves as a hunter and get their name, but if they are killed by someone, they
will lose one heart.

If someone has 15 hearts or more, the Karratos will begin.
Each death will remove three hearts.

If you lose all your hearts, you are banned.

You must be the last one to survive.

## Technologies

Server-side mod.

- Java 21
- Fabric API
